# YOUC Supported Countries and Regions

Effective Date: 2026-06-15  
Website: https://ai.youc.online

This document explains YOUC's service availability regions, restricted regions, and regional compliance requirements. This document is an integral part of the YOUC Terms of Service. As upstream service providers, payment channels, cloud services, sanctions, export controls, and local regulatory requirements may change, YOUC may update, restrict, or discontinue services in any country or region at any time.

## 1. General Principles

YOUC provides services only in countries and regions that simultaneously meet all of the following conditions:

1. The country or region is not part of Mainland China.
2. You, your organization, your billing address, payment source, IP address, device environment, principal place of business, actual controller, end users, and actual use are not in Mainland China.
3. The upstream service providers you call allow access, development, commercial use, and API client provision in that country or region.
4. The country or region is not subject to applicable sanctions, export controls, embargoes, anti-money laundering, anti-terrorist financing, or other transaction restrictions.
5. Your use does not require YOUC to obtain local permits, filings, registrations, security assessments, algorithm filings, generative AI service filings, payment licenses, industry qualifications, or other administrative approvals that we have not yet obtained.
6. YOUC has not listed the country or region as restricted based on compliance, risk control, security, payment, upstream requirements, or business judgment.

If any of the above conditions is not met, the country or region is not supported, even if the website is accessible, accounts can be registered, orders can be created, or payments can be completed. Such circumstances do not mean YOUC provides services to that country or region.

## 2. Mainland China Is Not Supported

YOUC does not provide services to the public in Mainland China, nor does it allow access to, purchase of, topping up of, calling of, resale of, integration with, promotion of, or substantial use of the Services from Mainland China.

You may not use YOUC to provide or indirectly provide the following services in Mainland China:

1. Generative artificial intelligence services.
2. Deep synthesis services.
3. Algorithm recommendation services.
4. Internet information services.
5. API relay, model call resale, credit distribution, shared subscriptions, paid calling, proxy top-up/payment, or similar services.
6. Any services that may require administrative permission, ICP filing, commercial ICP license, algorithm filing, security assessment, generative AI service filing, content safety review, personal information protection compliance, data export compliance, payment settlement compliance, or tax compliance in Mainland China.

If you are located in Mainland China, or if your product primarily targets users in Mainland China, or if Mainland China is your principal place of operation, registration, residence, benefit, billing, payment, server location, marketing, or end-user location, you may not use YOUC. If you use VPNs, proxies, overseas servers, overseas domains, overseas phone numbers, overseas payment accounts, or others' identities to circumvent this restriction, all consequences are your sole responsibility.

## 3. Upstream Service Provider Regional Requirements

Different YOUC channels may rely on different upstream service providers. You must simultaneously meet the supported countries and regions requirements of the corresponding upstream service providers. Where upstream policies are stricter than this document, the stricter rules prevail.

| Upstream or Function | Regional Determination Rules |
| --- | --- |
| OpenAI API-related channels | Limited to countries and territories supported by the official OpenAI API; accessing or providing access in unsupported regions may result in account blocking or suspension. |
| Anthropic / Claude-related channels | Limited to countries and regions officially supported by Anthropic and scenarios permitted by its supported regions policy. |
| Google AI Studio / Gemini API-related channels | Limited to regions where Google's official Gemini API / AI Studio is available; if providing API clients to users in the EEA, Switzerland, or the UK, you must also comply with Google's special requirements for paid services. |
| Other model, payment, or cloud service channels | Subject to the corresponding service provider's latest terms, region lists, sanctions, export controls, industry restrictions, and account rules. |

YOUC does not guarantee that all models, upstream providers, payment methods, or functions are available in any particular region. The availability of one model, channel, function, payment method, or account does not imply that others are also available.

## 4. Presumptively Unsupported Regions

Unless YOUC expressly permits in writing, the following regions, entities, or scenarios are not supported:

1. Mainland China.
2. Any country or region that is not supported, suspended, prohibited from access, or prohibited from providing access by any upstream service provider.
3. Countries, regions, governments, entities, individuals, vessels, institutions, or end uses covered by applicable sanctions, embargoes, export controls, or transaction restrictions, including but not limited to Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Crimea, Donetsk, Luhansk, Sevastopol, and other regions or entities listed on restricted lists when relevant sanctions rules apply.
4. Regions where YOUC is required to obtain local permits, filings, licenses, regulatory approvals, or localization obligations that YOUC has not yet obtained.
5. Regions with high fraud, high chargeback, high abuse, high regulatory risk, or unstable payment channels.
6. Any situation where you cannot provide proof of true identity, true location, true payment source, true end users, or true end purpose.

We may include any region, ASN, IP segment, payment method, email domain, phone number segment, device fingerprint, user group, or business scenario in the restricted scope.

## 5. Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan, and Other Special Regions

The Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, the Macao Special Administrative Region, the Taiwan region, and other regions with special regulatory or upstream availability differences do not automatically receive full service support simply because they are not part of Mainland China.

When using the Services in the above regions, you must still satisfy:

1. The upstream service provider you call explicitly supports that region.
2. Your payment method, billing address, account registration location, and actual use location comply with upstream and payment channel requirements.
3. The Services may not primarily target users in Mainland China, and may not be used to provide API relay, paid calling, generative AI, deep synthesis, or similar services to Mainland China.
4. You may not use entities, servers, domains, proxies, payment channels, or phone numbers in that region to circumvent Mainland China restrictions, upstream regional restrictions, or other compliance requirements.

## 6. Regional Identification and Proof

To determine regional compliance, we may comprehensively use the following information:

1. Registration data, login IP, call IP, device information, User-Agent, time zone, language, ASN, proxy/VPN/data center characteristics.
2. Billing address, payment account, payment channel, bank card BIN, receiving/payment region, chargeback records.
3. Phone number, email domain, organization data, place of business, KYC/KYB documents, tax information.
4. Server location, application name, website content, primary language, marketing channels, customer support records, end-user distribution.
5. Information provided by upstream service providers, payment institutions, cloud service providers, risk control vendors, rights holders, regulatory authorities, or law enforcement agencies.

If we cannot confirm that you meet regional requirements, or reasonably suspect that you are circumventing regional restrictions, we may refuse service, restrict calls, request supplemental proof, freeze balances, suspend subscriptions, close accounts, or refuse refunds.

You should continuously ensure that your location, organization location, end-user location, business target regions, payment source, and actual use comply with this document. If your regional status changes, or your product begins to reach unsupported regions, you must immediately stop the relevant use and notify us.

You may not claim to others that YOUC is available in unsupported regions, nor provide tutorials, tools, accounts, proxies, proxy top-ups, proxy payments, or technical support for circumventing regional restrictions.

Account bans, balance freezes, upstream penalties, payment chargebacks, regulatory investigations, administrative penalties, civil claims, criminal liability, data loss, or business losses caused by your violation of regional requirements are your sole responsibility, and you shall compensate YOUC for losses suffered as a result.
